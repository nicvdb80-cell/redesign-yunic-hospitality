Indonesia,
told at the table.

Yunic Dining is our love letter to the archipelago, regional Indonesian menus cooked in your home or villa, built from the morning market and served with the stories behind each dish.

Cuisine — The archipelago's regional voices Setting — Your home or villa Host — Yuli, telling the stories

Flavours · Memories · Voices

Every region speaks
its own language of flavour.

From the slow-built spice pastes of Java to the bright sambals of the islands and the ceremonial dishes of Bali, Indonesian food carries memory. Yunic Dining brings those voices to your table, one region, one season, one story at a time.

The menu is written after we talk: what you have tasted, what you are curious about, how adventurous the table feels. Then we go to the market, and the evening takes shape around what the island offers that day.

Yuli hosts the table and tells the stories, where a dish comes from, who taught it to us, why it tastes the way it does. Dinner becomes a journey without leaving your villa.
