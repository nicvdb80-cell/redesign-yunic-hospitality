A private chef experience with Yunic is not about performance. Our chef cooks in your space, with ingredients chosen for you, on a menu built around your occasion.

What is the setting? Who is coming? Is there a reason behind the evening, a celebration, a reunion, something quieter? From there we build something that fits. Not a standard menu adapted to your name, but a menu that begins with you.

Yuli takes care of everything around the table, the timing, the atmosphere, the small things that make a dinner feel genuinely hosted. You bring the people. We bring the rest.
