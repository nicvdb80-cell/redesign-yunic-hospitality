03 — About Yunic

Yunic exists because the most memorable evenings are never just about the food.

Yunic is Yuli's step forward. After years spent alongside a chef, listening, tasting, travelling, living the rhythm of kitchens from the other side of the pass, she decided to stop watching from the wings and run the business herself. She hosts every gathering and takes care of everything around the table: the timing, the atmosphere, the hundred small things guests remember long after the plates are cleared.

Behind the scenes, a trusted culinary team, decades of experience across restaurants, private dining and luxury resorts, takes care of what arrives on the table. And a chef partner, long part of the journey, will join forces when the time is right. Until then, the cooking simply speaks for itself.

We never arrive with a fixed script or a rehearsed performance. Every home, villa and celebration is different, and every host has a different vision. We take the time to understand the occasion, adapt to the setting and become a natural part of it, always respecting the rhythm of your guests and the atmosphere you want to create.

Whether it is an intimate dinner, a weekend with family or a larger event, our aim is always the same: to leave you free to enjoy the moment while we quietly take care of everything behind the scenes. That, to us, is what hospitality should feel like.

Years of listening, tasting and travelling, now poured into a business of her own.
